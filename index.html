Affordable Apartments in Athens

Apartment searching in Athens, Georgia may yield cheaper results than in other cities, but that does not mean the process of apartment hunting is any simpler. In fact, due to the greater influx of college-aged students in the area it can be even harder to find and settle on an apartment that meets one’s standards before all availabilities are completely filled. As far as price and likely availability go, the ideal apartment style would be a four bedroom apartment with separate, fixed rents for each roommate. Under this assumption, the best deal apartments in Athens for the Fall of 2017 have been narrowed down to four main communities: The Park, River Club, and Abbey West. Closely examined, each have merits and detriments that should be presented before any application process begins.

The Park

At The Park, four bedroom apartments start leasing at $365 but can reach up to $405 depending on room style and number of baths. Their apartment amenities range from fully furnished rooms, Internet, cable TV and water/sewer included, in-unit laundry, hardwood-style floors, a full kitchen with microwave and oven, as well as other perks. Their community amenities range from a fitness center, two pools, hosting socials to encourage relationships between people in in the community, a bus service to UGA, and quite a few other benefits. The Park has a decent 3.2 rating on Google Reviews, which for a college-geared apartment complex is fairly average.

Notable Merits
  • Includes Internet, cable TV, and water in rent
  • Cheapest option on the list
  • Has longer lease than usual
Notable Detriments
  • Has the lowest Google Review on list
  • Many complaints about management

River Club

River Club offers four bedroom four bath apartments starting at $405. Their apartment amenities range from a flat panel HDTV in all living rooms, fully furnished rooms, wi-fi and water included, a kitchen with a dishwasher, and a choice between townhouse style or apartment style housing. Their community amenities range from being a gated community, having a car wash station, tennis, basketball, and volleyball courts, a bus to UGA. River Club scores an above average 3.4 on Google Reviews. Additionally, currently all signing fees are waived including application fees, the annual fee, and the deposit fee. They are also offering a $150 gift card through the month of April.

Abbey West

With Abbey West, one will find four bedroom apartments beginning at $390 if it is unfurnished and $420 if it is furnished. Their apartment amenities include breakfast bars in kitchen, electronic front door locks, mirror closet doors, personal alarms, large closets, and other privileges. Their community amenities include monthly resident events, continental breakfast, complimentary lemonade, cookies, and coffee, batting cage, dog park, and several other inclusions. Abbey West receives a 3.3 on Google Reviews.

Notable Merits
  • Has complimentary food
  • Serves breakfast for its residents
  • Very pet friendly
Notable Detriments
  • Has been noted for charging overages
  • Rent is not fixed
  • Does not include or offer furniture, cable, or Internet at all
